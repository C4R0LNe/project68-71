John Burroughs Middle School

Campus Life

Established in 1924

John Burroughs Middle School first opened its doors in 1924 with 400 students and 23 teachers. Today, there are over 2,000 students enrolled in grades six, seven and eight. The school has consistently maintained a prestigious reputation for academic excellence and the cultural diversity found here provides a rich educational environment for its students.

Awards

As a recipient of the 2018 National Magnet Schools of Excellence, the 2018 Educational Results Partnership (ERP) Honor Roll, the 2017 California Gold Ribbon Schools Award, and the 2017 Title 1 Academic Achievement Award, our commitment to meeting the needs of the whole child has never been stronger.
